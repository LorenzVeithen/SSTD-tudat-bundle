\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class to compute typical relativistic corrections to the dynamics of an orbiter. 

Class to compute typical relativistic corrections to the dynamics of an orbiter. The model allows the inclusion of the Schwarzschild, Lense-\/\+Thirring and de Sitter terms (any one of them may be turned on and off). An excellent introduction to these models is given in \textquotesingle{}General relativity and Space Geodesy\textquotesingle{} by L. Combrinck (2012).
