\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class for calculating the radiation pressure acceleration on a panelled body. 

Class for calculating the radiation pressure acceleration on a panelled body, with the force due to each panel calculated from its area, orientation and emissivity. The emissivity determines the fraction that is absorbed (modelled as a force in line with the vector to th source) and one from reflection (modelled as a force normal to the panel).
