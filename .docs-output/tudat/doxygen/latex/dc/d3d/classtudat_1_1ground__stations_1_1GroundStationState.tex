Set of unit vectors of topocentric frame. 

Set of unit vectors of topocentric frame, expressed in body-\/fixed frame, in ENU (Earth-\/\+North-\/\+Up) order. The up-\/vector is generated by drawing a line from the center of the body to the (nominal) position of the station.
