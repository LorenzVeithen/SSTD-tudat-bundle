class tudat\+::observation\+\_\+models\+::\+One\+Way\+Doppler\+Observation\+Model$<$ Observation\+Scalar\+Type, Time\+Type $>$}

Computes observable the (simplified) one-\/way Doppler observation between two link ends, omitting proper time rates and light time corrections.

Computes observable the (simplified) one-\/way Doppler observation between two link ends, omitting proper time rates and light time corrections. The observable is defined as d f\+\_\+\{B\}/d\+\_\+f\+\_\+\{A\} -\/ 1, with A the transmitter and B the receiver, and f the frequency of the signal.
